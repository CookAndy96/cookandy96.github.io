Physical and Observable Properties of the Circumgalactic Medium

The CGM is a complex, multiphase region meaning the gas spans a wide range of temperatures. In this research, we focused on analysing the dependence of the physical properties, including temperature, density, metallicity and radial velocity, and observable properties, such as column density, with the mass of the dark matter halo.
